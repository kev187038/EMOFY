Help Center

Welcome to the Emofy help center! This guide will help you understand how to use our image analysis service efficiently.

Uploading Images

To upload images, you can:

  • Drag and drop them into the designated upload area on the home page
  • Click inside the upload box to select images from your device

Supported image formats include JPG, PNG, and other common formats. After uploading, only images containing faces will be stored in your account for further analysis.

Your Images

Once images are uploaded, they will appear in the "Your Images" section. Here, you can:

  • Select an image to view the results of the facial expression recognition model
  • Correct the model's prediction using a dropdown list if it doesn't match the actual expression

Your feedback is valuable and helps improve the model's accuracy over time. Currently, the model's prediction accuracy exceeds 60%.

Editing Images

For each image, you have the option to apply various filters. To modify an image:

  1. Select the desired filters from the dropdown menu
  2. Adjust the filters as needed
  3. Click "Store Result" to save the modified image in your library

Managing Images

To remove an image from your library:

  1. Select the image you want to delete
  2. Click the "Delete Image" button

Be aware that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
